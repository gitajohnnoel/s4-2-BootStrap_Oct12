Bootstrap has its own responsive paddings and margins Wherein its classes, m stands for margin mt mb ml mr, p stands for padding
Bootstrap Padding and Margins can be applied on a specific sides
  • t - sets either margin or padding at the top of our elements: mt or pt
  • b - sets either margin or padding at the bottom side of our element: mb or pb
  • y - sets either margin or padding on y-axis(both top and bottom): my or py
  • x - sets either margin or padding on x-axis(both left and right): mx or px
  • l - sets either margin or padding at the left of our elements: ml or pl
  • r - sets either margin or padding at the right side of our element: mr or pr
  • blank - sets the margin on all sides: m or p
